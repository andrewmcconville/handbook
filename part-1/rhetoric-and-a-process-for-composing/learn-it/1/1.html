People who study writers and writing have learned that all effective writers follow processes to develop writing. These processes differ in their particulars from writer to writer, but generally - when it comes to composing a research paper - the processess contain the steps shown on the next page.

(Keep in mind that, although the process looks linear, writers often move up and down among the steps - especially between revising and getting feedback.)

With each step you become more certain about your audience, context, purpose and - eventually - strategies.
