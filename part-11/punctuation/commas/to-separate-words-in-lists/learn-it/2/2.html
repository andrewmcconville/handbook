comma use 3

to separate words in lists

The Pattern

Only two nouns? No commas.

Three or more nouns? Commas!

I like sandwiches with ham and cheese.

I like sandwiches with ham, cheese, and pickles.

Check out Louis C.K.’s pallor and eyebags.

Check out Louis C.K.’s pallor, pudge, and eyebags.

A child is a territory and a landscape.

A child is a territory, a landscape, a republic, and an island of worry.

Other writers who scholars have argued had temporal lobe epilepsy include Tennyson and Lear.

Other writers who scholars have argued had temporal lobe epilepsy include Tennyson, Lear, Poe,
Byron, Molière, Pascal, Teresa of Avila, and Saint Paul.
