comma use 3

to separate words in lists

The Pattern

Only two phrases or clauses? No commas.

Three or more phrases or clauses? Commas!

Gilligan’s research emphasizes relationships over rules and connection over isolation.

Gilligan’s research emphasizes relationships over rules, connection over isolation, caring over violence, and a web of relationships over hierarchy.

When our desires are left unchecked they lead to broken relationships and forest fires.

When our desires are left unchecked they lead to broken relationships, prison time, and forest fires.
