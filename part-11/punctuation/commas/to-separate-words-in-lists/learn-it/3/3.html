comma use 3

to separate words in lists

The Pattern

Only two verbs? No commas.

Three or more verbs? Commas!

Infants eat and sleep.

Infants eat, sleep, and fill their diapers.

A naturalist must observe and be quiet.

A naturalist must observe, listen, smell, touch, and be quiet.

People who Parkour vault and jump through a city’s streets and walls.

People who Parkour vault, roll, run, climb, and jump through a city’s streets and walls.

Lawrence North High School basketball center Greg Oden passes and scores.

Lawrence North High School basketball center Greg Oden passes, blocks, shoots, rebounds, and smiles.
