The Pattern

Only two nouns, verbs, adverbs, adjectives, phrases, or clauses in a sentence? No commas.

Three or more nouns, verbs, adverbs, adjectives, phrases, or clauses in a sentence? Commas!

I like sandwiches with ham and cheese.

I like sandwiches with ham, cheese, and pickles.

Infants eat and sleep.

Infants eat, sleep, and fill their diapers.

Plain and simple language is often effective.

Plain, simple, short, and quick language is often effective.

She walks softly and quietly.

She walks softly, quietly, and wickedly.
